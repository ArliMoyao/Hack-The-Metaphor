Hack the Metaphor

The Hack the Metaphor card game is based off of the paper, Hacking Metaphors in the Anticipatory Governance of Emerging Technology: The Case of Regulating Robots written by Meg Leta Jones and Jason Millar. Jones and Millar discuss how metaphors are critical tools that shape the way people interpret new technologies. The ways which new technologies are described impacts our perceptions, and impacts the ways they are designed, used, a nd governed. Hack the Metaphor is a game for all age groups with the intent of interrogating the metaphors we use, and challenging them to make sure they represent the world we would like to create.
